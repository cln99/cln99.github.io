JAM PAGE

Boom was the second game I made this term, I made the main part of this game in about 2 hours and then added on graphics and finished up the rest.

Boom was inspired by Keep Talking and Nobody Explodes, a two player game where one person is defusing a bomb and the other person is giving them instructions on how to defuse it. I decided to make a version of that but single player, which makes it more about quickly reading and understand information rather than communication between two people.

Due to time constraints and to keep things simplistic I only have 2 different modules to defuse, a button and wires. The ways to defuse them are random each time forcing the player to quickly read the manual.

While I did some polish on the graphics, I'm not really happy with them and would redo them in a different style if I had the time.

While this game was "finished" in a way, I didn't really want to release it as I just didn't feel it was good enough. There isn't a lot to the gameplay, the graphics aren't great and I just wasn't happy with it.
