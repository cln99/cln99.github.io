Mini Jam 118: Vampires

JAM PAGE

This was the first game I decided to work on this term, originally more as something to get me back into the game making spririt as I hadn't really worked on a game in what felt like forever.

As expected with that I didn't end up finishing this game, I had vague ideas that I didn't know how to implement and little to no motivation during development.

Despite that I did manage to get a little bit done and the idea I had for the game was somewhat interesting. The core idea is that you play as a vampire who is allergic to blood, so without blood you die, with too much blood you die.

The idea was that you would slowly lose blood overtime and would have to drain enemies blood to stay alive, but manage it so that you wouldn't have too much blood. You would also have two different "modes", vampire mode and bat mode. Vampire has more limited movement but gives more blood on kill, while Bat can fly but your blood drains faster while in it and you get less blood from enemies while in Bat mode.

While this was a neat idea I wasn't sure what sort of level to put this in, during development I decided on a cave system but looking back this was a poor choice for this kind of game. I should've gone with a more Spelunky style level generation instead where the level is randomly placed premade rooms. This would've made enemy placement a lot easier to implement and overall fit the gameplay much better.

Despite it not really working with the game, I am happy with the level generation system as it was my first time properly using it and it works fairly well. I might end up recycling this code in the future if I need a level gen system like it.
