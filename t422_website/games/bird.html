Bird Jam

JAM PAGE

I wanted to keep myself to doing shorter jams to keep myself on track during development, unfortunately there aren't that many short jams at the moment so I've ended up doing a few longer jams, just restricting myself to shorter time periods.

Bird Jam is a jam where your game has to be related to birds in some way, I decided to make it a relaxed platformer about a flightless bird looking for a new nesting place. Since you play as a flightless bird you only have a short hop and can slow your fall, I didn't want there to be much difficulty outside of the puzzle parts.

I made some alright animations for the bird and the environment I made actually looks kinda nice, although I didn't end up doing much with it. I didn't really have much of an idea for the puzzles and ended up scrapping this game when I got to that point.
