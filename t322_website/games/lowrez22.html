LOW REZ JAM 2022

JAM PAGE

LOW REZ JAM is a 14 day gamejam where the only restriction is that your game must be in a 64x64 pixel resolution or lower.

This adds quite a bit of extra challenge to making the game since you have to fit everything into such a tiny space.

My original idea for this jam was to make a top down shooter similar to Binding Of Isaac or Nuclear Throne. I think a big part of why I didn't end up finishing the game had to do with not having any other ideas or anything interesting to add to it.

I started working on my game and added a basic top down controller and started working on a random map generator.

There's plenty of ways to generate random levels but I thought a good way for this game would be a Spelunky style generation, where you stitch a bunch of premade rooms together to make something unique.

I ended up coding a generator where I could put in an image of rooms and it would be able to generate a map using them.

This was my first time implementing a system like this and I'm pretty proud of how well it works.

Unfortunately I wasn't really sure what to do next and didn't feel motivated to work on the game.

I let it sit for a bit and on the 2nd week of the jam I restarted as I didn't see what to do with what I had.
