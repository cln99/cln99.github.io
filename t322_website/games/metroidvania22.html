METROIDVANIA JAM 2022

JAM PAGE

Metroidvania Month 15 is a month long jam with the only restriction being to make a metroidvania game.

A metroidvania game is a typically 2d side-scrolling platformer where you explore a big map where you slowly find upgrades and beat bosses.

Lasting a month, this is the longest jam I signed up for and the ending day ended up in exhibition week, I originally thought this was perfect as it meant I could release it on that day as a month long project.

Unfortunately I didn't end up finishing this jam, as it being so close to exhibitions meant that I had to split it with all the other work I needed to get done. As well as being a generally larger project than the other games.

My original idea was to make a somewhat generic metroidvania with the gimmick being that you had to control two characters at once.

I did a little bit of work on this prototype but grew bored of it pretty quickly for whatever reason.

I restarted this one and started working on a FPS metroidvania set in an urban city.

I didn't get too far on this one only getting a small part of the city and some basic player movement. From what's there it looks pretty good though.
