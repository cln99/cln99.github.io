LOW REZ JAM 2022

JAM PAGE

LOW REZ JAM is a 14 day gamejam where the only restriction is that your game must be in a 64x64 pixel resolution or lower.

This covers my second game attempt for the LOW REZ JAM 22, starting over again I decided to make a simplistic 3D platformer.

I kinda just started working on it without planning that much, I made a little starting zone and managed to find a nice looking aesthetic.

I really liked this style and might use it in future 3D games as it looks sharp, nice and low effort.

Gameplay wise it's pretty simplistic, I couldn't think of anything interesting to add as a gimmick and the game kinda fell apart because of that.
