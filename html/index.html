Hello! Welcome to Soia Lindberg's Big Picture portfolio!

Soia Lindberg is a Year 12 Big Picture graduate of Five Islands Secondary College -- with primary interests in software/web development, 19th century baseball history, game development.

My main work this past year has been a detailed history of early baseball (1843-1876), focusing on the early organisations and culture that built the game, and those that attempted to guide it through the developments of commercialism and professionalism.
