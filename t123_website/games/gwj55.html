Godot Wild Jam 55

JAM PAGE

Godot Wild Jam is a monthly jam exclusively for entries using Godot. It lasts a little more than a week.

I wasn't quite sure what to do for this jam, although I had a vague idea of marble or some kind of sphere related gameplay. Inspired by games like Super Monkey Ball and the Marble series, I decided to make a game about rotating a stage to get marbles through a course into a goal.

I decided to make the game in 3D since doing it 2D would've taken way longer. I was able to get a decent amount done, and practically finished the game, just without any polish really.

As it turns out by the time I finished the core game I didn't really have any time to polish or work on it anymore so unfortunately I had to leave it unfinished.
